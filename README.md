# 📚 Social Studies Assessments

A clean, modern web-based assessment platform for social studies courses. Easy to use online or download for offline access.

## 🚀 Download

1. Click the green **<> Code** button ↗️ at the top right
2. Select **Download ZIP**
3. Extract the folder to your computer
4. Double-click **index.html** to open in your browser
5. Done! Responses save automatically.

## 📋 Assessments Available

- **Unit 4: Modern Geopolitics** - The Cold War and Beyond
  - 5 questions mixing multiple choice and short answer
  - Auto-saves your progress

## ✨ Features

✅ **Auto-Save** - Your answers save automatically as you type  
✅ **Offline Ready** - Download and use without internet  
✅ **Mobile Friendly** - Works on phones, tablets, and computers  
✅ **Print Friendly** - Can be printed as a PDF  
✅ **No Installation** - Just open in your browser  

## 🎓 For Instructors

**Sharing with students:**
- Download the ZIP file and email it to students
- Students extract and open index.html in any browser
- Works on any computer, no installation needed

## 🔧 Technical Details

- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript - no backend needed
- Uses browser LocalStorage for response saving
- Fully responsive design
- Works offline

## 📝 Notes

- Responses are saved in your browser's local storage
- Clear your browser cache to reset responses
- Each browser/device stores responses separately
